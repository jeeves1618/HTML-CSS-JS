Chicken

It's a classic for good reason, this chicken salad recipe combines chicken, mayo, mustard, a dash of hot sauce, and a squeeze of lemon juice with minced red onion, celery, and parsley. You can use leftover roast chicken, rotisserie chicken, or any chicken you have for this delicious lunch. Try it on a bed of mixed greens or a slice of whole-grain of bread.

Beef

Beef Wellington is a beef tenderloin coated with mushrooms, onions, and liver. Puff pastry encases the beef Wellington, making the outside buttery and crispy. When shopping for this beef Wellington recipe, look for duck liver, chicken liver, or peppercorn mousse in specialty-food shops. Try it on a bed of greens or a slice of whole-grain of bread.
